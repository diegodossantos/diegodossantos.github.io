Arrow of Time

I rememeber seeing Diego Goldber's Arrow of Time photo series for the first time, back when I was around 15 years old in Rio. I didn't even have a camera at that time, but I knew one day I'd do it with my family. Fast forward some 10 years, and a few months after I got married, this project came back to me. So now, every year on our wedding anniversary, we take a photo of each one of us, to register for a moment, as Diego Golderg puts it, "the arrow of time passing by."
