About me

I’m a software product and program manager living in Asheville, NC.

I was in social media marketing for the first 4 years of my career doing a variety of things. This is when I started building digital experiences. I spent the following 7 years growing as a product manager on the business side. I started by managing the social experiences for EMC and Dell's digital events. I then led the B2B lead gen sales chat application on dell.com, focusing on the experience for both end customers as well as for a global team of sales agents. During this time, I developed a chat bot, expanded to messaging on WhatsApp, developed automations, ran 20+ experiments and collaborated on the consolidation of the B2B and consumer sales chat.

While I'm in tech, other industries I'd enjoy are renewables, media, agrotech, and fintech.

When I’m not working, I’m usually outside with my wife and 3 boys. I’m into hiking, backpacking, mountain biking, paddle boarding, snowboarding, fly fishing, trail running and photography.

I’m also interested in my long-term health through exercise, eating well, getting enough sleep, and mindfulness.
